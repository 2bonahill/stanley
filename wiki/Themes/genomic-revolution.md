# Genomic Revolution (ARKG)

> The intersection of targeted therapeutics, bioinformatics, and molecular diagnostics.
> **Benchmark ETF:** [ARKG](ARKG.md)

---

## Core Thesis
The "Genomic Revolution" is driven by the convergence of DNA sequencing, CRISPR gene editing, and AI-driven biological data analysis. This enables personalized medicine, early cancer detection (MRD), and cures for previously untreatable genetic diseases.

---

## Key Technologies

### 1. Gene Editing (CRISPR)
Precision modification of the human genome to treat or cure diseases at the source.
- **Leaders:** [CRSP](CRSP.md), [BEAM](BEAM.md), [NTLA](NTLA.md), [PRME](PRME.md)

### 2. Molecular Diagnostics & MRD
Non-invasive liquid biopsies for early cancer detection and minimal residual disease (MRD) monitoring.
- **Leaders:** [NTRA](NTRA.md), [VCYT](VCYT.md), [PSNL](PSNL.md), [GH](GH.md)

### 3. AI-Driven Drug Discovery
Using machine learning and massive multimodal datasets to design novel antibodies and small molecules.
- **Leaders:** [TEM](TEM.md), [ABSI](ABSI.md), [RXRX](RXRX.md), [SDGR](SDGR.md)

### 4. Synthetic Biology & Sequencing
The infrastructure of the revolution: tools to read (sequencing) and write (synthesis) DNA.
- **Leaders:** [TWST](TWST.md), [TXG](TXG.md), [ILMN](ILMN.md), [PACB](PACB.md)
