# Spatial Biology & Single-Cell Analysis

## Overview
Spatial biology and single-cell analysis allow researchers to examine the cellular and molecular organization of tissues in their native context. Unlike "bulk" sequencing, which grinds up tissue and loses location data, these tools preserve the "map" of where each cell is and what it is doing.

## Why Interesting
- **Precision Medicine:** Essential for understanding tumor microenvironments and autoimmune responses.
- **AI Data Platform:** High-resolution biological data is the highest-value training set for next-gen biological AI models.
- **Reagent Pull-Through:** Large install bases of expensive instruments ($250k-$1M+) create durable, high-margin consumable revenue streams.
- **Clinical Transition:** The sector is currently moving from pure academic discovery into clinical diagnostics.
