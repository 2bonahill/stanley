# Theme: Space Technology & Orbital AI
> **Focus:** The vertical integration of low-earth orbit (LEO) infrastructure, daily global intelligence, and orbital AI compute.

---

## 🚀 Thesis
Space is no longer just about launch. The second wave is about the "Orbital Edge"—treating satellites as remote AI sensors and compute nodes. Companies that control the daily data stream (imagery) and the hardware layer (optical links, TPUs in orbit) will become the sovereign intelligence backbones for the next decade.

## 🏗️ The Stack
1.  **The Eyes (Imagery):** Daily global views. Leader: [PL](../Companies/PL.md).
2.  **The Brain (Orbital AI):** Onboard inference + datacenters. Partner: [PL](../Companies/PL.md) (Suncatcher).
3.  **The Backbone (Comms):** Truly global L-band network. Leader: [IRDM](../Companies/IRDM.md).
4.  **The Infrastructure:** Launch, manufacturing, and subsystems. [RKLB](../Companies/RKLB.md), [RDW](../Companies/RDW.md).
