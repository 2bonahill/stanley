# Portable Nuclear Energy (Microreactors)

## Overview
Next-generation nuclear fission technology focused on "microreactors" (1-20 MW) that are factory-built, transportable via truck/shipping container, and designed for off-grid or specialized power needs.

## Why Interesting
- AI Power Wall: Hyperscale data centers are running out of grid power; microreactors offer a localized, zero-carbon solution.
- Regulatory Shift: NRC and DOE are creating accelerated pathways for SMRs/MMRs.
- Deep Moat: Massive regulatory and engineering barriers to entry.
- Vertical Integration: Companies capturing the fuel (HALEU) and transport loop have a multi-layered moat.
