# Defense Innovation

## One-Line Summary
The convergence of autonomous systems, LEO constellations, and AI-driven deterrence transforming modern warfare.

---

## The ARKX "Defense Innovation" Pivot (Late 2025)
In November 2025, the **ARK Space Exploration & Innovation ETF** officially rebranded to the **ARK Space & Defense Innovation ETF**. This shift reflects the reality that "Space" and "Defense" are now technologically inseparable.

### Key Investment Pillars
1.  **Autonomous Platforms**: Low-cost, high-attrition drones (AVAV, KTOS, ONDS).
2.  **LEO Intelligence**: Real-time geospatial and communications backbone (PL, IRDM, BKSY).
3.  **Kinetic Modernization**: Hypersonics and portable nuclear power for remote bases (NNE, BWXT).
4.  **AI Deterrence**: Software-defined warfare and predictive analytics (PLTR, MSFT, AMD).
