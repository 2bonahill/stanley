# Theme: AI Automation & Agentic AI
> **Focus:** The transition from passive software tools to autonomous agents that observe, reason, and act within enterprise workflows.

---

## 🚀 Thesis
The first wave of AI (Generative) focused on content creation. The second wave (Agentic) focuses on execution. Companies with deep access to existing process data and the "action layer" (UI automation, API integration) are the primary winners.

## 🏗️ The Stack
1. **The Brain:** LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google).
2. **The Context:** Enterprise process data (UiPath, ServiceNow, Salesforce).
3. **The Action:** UI/API Automation (UiPath, Microsoft Power Automate).

## 📊 Companies Tracked
- **[PATH](../Companies/PATH.md):** The leader in enterprise RPA transitioning to Multi-Agent Systems (MAS).
- **[U](../Companies/U.md):** Using AI (Vector) to optimize ad-monetization and RT3D creation.
- **MSFT:** (Mega Cap) The dominant challenger via Power Automate and Copilot.
- **NOW:** (Large Cap) ServiceNow, integrating agentic workflows into IT/HR.

## 📡 Signals & Catalysts
- **Multi-Agent Swarms:** Shift from one bot to "swarms" of specialized agents.
- **Governance-as-Code:** The need for compliance in autonomous execution.
- **The "Great Replace":** AI agents replacing low-value manual processing in Finance/HR/Legal.
