# Theme: Rare & Endocrine Diseases

## 2026 Outlook

The "Oral Revolution" in endocrinology is the primary theme for 2026. CRNX's PALSONIFY launch is the bellwether. Success there validates the patient preference for oral options over traditional monthly injections (octreotide/lanreotide). EWTX represents the next wave of functional protection in muscular dystrophies.
