# Multimodal AI Diagnostics

## Overview
The convergence of massive biological datasets (DNA, RNA, protein, images) with generative AI to create predictive healthcare models.

## Why Interesting
- **Data Moats:** Companies like Tempus have petabytes of proprietary clinical data that cannot be replicated.
- **Predictive Power:** Shifting from "what is the mutation" to "how will this patient respond to drug X."
