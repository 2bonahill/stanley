# Biotech — Antibody Discovery Platform

## Overview
Companies building proprietary platforms to discover, engineer, and develop antibody-based medicines. Focus is on founder-led companies with defensible IP in antibody screening, selection, and optimization technology — distinct from pure contract research or commodity antibody manufacturing.

## Why Interesting
Antibody drugs are the dominant modality in modern pharmacology — monoclonal antibodies, bispecifics, and ADCs represent the majority of blockbuster approvals. The picks-and-shovels layer (discovery infrastructure) has enormous royalty tail potential: a single successful partner program can generate hundreds of millions in milestones and royalties. Founder-led companies with patented platform technology and internal pipelines represent the best of both worlds — platform royalties + clinical upside.
