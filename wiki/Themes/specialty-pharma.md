## 2026 Outlook

Consolidation and margin expansion are the goals. INDV is transitioning leadership to focus on commercial scaling of Sublocade. AMRX is fighting through a high debt load by shifting to higher-margin biosimilars. LQDA is the disruptor, moving from a litigation-heavy story to a commercial story in PH.
