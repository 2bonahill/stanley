# Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS)

## Overview
The "Genomic Era" is powered by the ability to read, write, and edit DNA at scale. Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) is the underlying engine, shifting from expensive research tools to ubiquitous clinical diagnostics.

## Why Interesting
- **Decline in Cost:** Sequencing cost per genome has fallen from $100M to <$200.
- **Ubiquity:** NGS is becoming the standard of care for oncology, rare disease, and reproductive health.
- **Multimodal Era:** Transition from just DNA sequencing to multiomics (proteomics, transcriptomics).
