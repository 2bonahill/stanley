# Precision Diagnostics & Molecular Insights

## Overview
Precision diagnostics leverage genomic, proteomic, and transcriptomic data to provide high-resolution clarity on disease states, enabling "personalized medicine." This sector is moving from pure academic tools into clinical standards of care, replacing invasive tissue biopsies with molecular "liquid biopsies" or advanced genomic profiling.

## Why Interesting
- **High Margins:** Specialized clinical tests often command 70%+ gross margins.
- **Switching Costs:** Once a diagnostic becomes a clinical gold standard (e.g., Decipher for prostate cancer), it is deeply embedded in physician workflows and insurance reimbursement policies.
- **Next-Gen Growth:** Minimal Residual Disease (MRD) monitoring is a massive emerging market, allowing for early detection of cancer recurrence.
- **Platform Expansion:** Companies with large genomic datasets can expand into drug discovery partnerships or multi-indication testing platforms.

## Key Trends
- **2026-04**: MRD launch wave. Veracyte (TrueMRD) and others are racing to validate clinical utility for post-treatment monitoring.
- **AI Integration**: Using machine learning to identify complex molecular signatures that human pathologists might miss.
- **Consolidation**: Larger diagnostic players acquiring niche platform technologies to build end-to-end oncology menus.
