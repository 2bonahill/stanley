# Synthetic Biology

## Overview
Synthetic biology (synbio) involves redesigning organisms for useful purposes by engineering them to have new abilities. This includes "writing" DNA, engineering cells to produce high-value chemicals/proteins, and developing new therapeutic modalities.

## Why Interesting
- The "Biological Century": Biology is becoming an engineering discipline.
- Unit economics improving: Cost of DNA synthesis falling faster than Moore's Law.
- Massive TAM: Spans biopharma, food, materials, and data storage.
- Resilience: Infrastructure providers (Twist) are showing durable business models even as "platform" companies (Ginkgo) struggle.
