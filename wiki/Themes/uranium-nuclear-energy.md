# Uranium & Nuclear Energy

## Overview
The "Nuclear Renaissance" is driven by massive AI data center power demand, decarbonization goals, and energy security. This theme covers the entire fuel cycle—from uranium mining and enrichment to next-generation Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) and microreactors.

## Why Interesting
- **AI Power Demand:** Hyperscalers (Microsoft, Amazon, Google) are pivoting to nuclear to provide 24/7 carbon-free baseload power.
- **Supply Deficit:** Structural uranium deficit after a decade of underinvestment; McArthur River and Cigar Lake ramping up but the market remains tight.
- **Geopolitics:** Shift away from Russian enrichment (HALEU) to Western sources (Centrus, Energy Fuels).
- **Scale:** SMRs offer a faster, more modular deployment path than traditional large-scale reactors.
