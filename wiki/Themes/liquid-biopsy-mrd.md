# Liquid Biopsy & MRD

## Overview
Liquid Biopsy (blood-based cancer detection) and Minimal Residual Disease (MRD) monitoring represent the most significant shift in oncology: from reactive treatment of large tumors to proactive detection of molecular signatures.

## Why Interesting
- **Asymmetric Growth:** MRD volume is growing at >50% YoY as it becomes a mandatory endpoint for drug trials.
- **Recurring Revenue:** Unlike one-time genomic tests, MRD monitoring is performed repeatedly (quarterly or bi-annually) for survivors.
- **Early Detection:** The "Holy Grail" of multi-cancer early detection (MCED).
