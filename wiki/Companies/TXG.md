**MISSION** — Accelerate the mastery of biology to advance human health by providing high-resolution discovery tools that enable single-cell and spatial analysis of life.

**TEAM** — Founder-led by CEO Serge Saxonov (founding architect at 23andMe) and CSO Ben Hindson; operational risk lies in successfully pivoting from a pure research tool company to a clinical diagnostics and AI data platform.

## Part 0 — The Investment Case (2026-04-16)

### 1. MISSION
10x Genomics aims to "master biology" by providing the tools that allow scientists to see biology at the resolution it actually functions—single cells and specific spatial locations. This is a fundamental shift from "bulk" genomics, creating a new standard for biological research.

### 2. TEAM & DRIVE
Co-founders Serge Saxonov and Ben Hindson have a track record of building category-defining life science tools (QuantaLife, acquired by Bio-Rad). They are mission-driven, as evidenced by their massive R&D reinvestment (frequently >30% of revenue) and a patent portfolio of thousands of grants.

### 3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS
- **Chromium:** The industry standard for single-cell analysis.
- **Visium:** Spatial transcriptomics (tissue mapping).
- **Xenium:** In situ analysis (high-speed, high-resolution mapping).
Management's goal is to transition these from "discovery only" to "clinical and AI" enablers, as seen in recent partnerships with Dana-Farber and Cancer Research Institute.

### 4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS
- **Core Revenue Growth:** 15%+ (returning from current flat-to-low single digits).
- **Gross Margin:** Stable or expanding at 68–70%.
- **FCF:** Consistent profitability as R&D investment levels off relative to revenue scale.
- **Xenium Install Base:** >1,000 units (currently ramping).

### 5. PRICE CATALYSTS
- **Q1/Q2 2026 Earnings:** First signs of instrument sales recovery following the 2025 "funding winter."
- **AI Partnership Milestones:** Concrete revenue or data-access deals with major pharma for "AI-ready" datasets.
- **Clinical Breakthrough:** First 10x-based diagnostic assay receiving regulatory clearance or clinical adoption.

### 6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR
Current Price: **~$25**. Green band entry: **<$22**. Valuation basis: **EV/Gross Profit < 6x**, which is historically near the floor for dominant life science tool providers.

### 7. THESIS KILLER
One sentence only. A competitor achieves comparable resolution and throughput at a significantly lower price point, or academic/government funding cycles remain structurally depressed for >24 months.

### 8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS
TXG is the dominant leader in a "biological writing" era where single-cell resolution is mandatory, not optional. The market is currently pricing it as a low-growth hardware company due to temporary macro headwinds, ignoring the massive platform optionality in clinical diagnostics and AI-training data.
