# AST SpaceMobile ($ASTS) 🔶 LARGE CAP

## Part 0 — Investment Case

**Mission Signal: STRONG** — Connecting every smartphone on Earth to cellular broadband from space — without any special hardware — is one of the most ambitious and clearly beneficial technology missions in public markets.

### 1. Mission
Build the first and only space-based cellular broadband network that connects directly to standard smartphones — bringing mobile internet to the billions of people in coverage gaps globally.

### 2. Team & Drive
Abel Avellan (B.S. Electrical Engineering; 25+ years satellite industry; founded and sold Emerging Markets Communications for $550M) is sole founder and Chairman/CEO. Holds 24 U.S. patents personally. However: CTO Yao Huiwen sold 40,000 shares (Mar 2026, $3.56M); American Tower sold 2.29M shares (Dec 2025). CEO Avellan has prepaid forward contracts — structured monetization, not open-market buying. Director Larson bought minimal 625 shares. Net insider activity is selling-dominant.

### 3. Products / Services / Goals
- **BlueBird 1–5 (Block 1)** — 5 commercial LEO satellites; voice/video/5G from space demonstrated; proof of concept at scale
- **Block 2 satellites** — 10x data capacity (2,400 sq ft array, 10 GHz processing, 120 Mbps peak); commercial-scale product; launches 2026–2027
- **45–60 satellite constellation** — target EOY 2026 for partial commercial service
- **50+ mobile operator partnerships** — AT&T, Vodafone, Orange, Telefónica, TELUS; pre-agreements to route traffic via ASTS
