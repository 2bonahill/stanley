## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Build the world's most trusted contract manufacturer for photonic and optical components, serving the AI infrastructure and telecom backbone markets through a non-compete model that makes customers share their most sensitive designs.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Seamus Grady (ex-Sanmina) has run Fabrinet since 2008. Track record: consistent margin expansion, 42% ROIC, 8 consecutive earnings beats. Not founder-led but a long-tenured operator with strong capital discipline. Risk: $30M+ insider selling with zero open-market purchases over 18 months.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Fabrinet is a pure-play optical contract manufacturer (OCM) headquartered in Bangkok, Thailand. Unlike standard EMS (electronics manufacturing services), Fabrinet focuses exclusively on precision optical, electronic, and electromechanical assemblies. Key distinction: the non-compete model — Fabrinet never competes with its customers, so customers trust it with proprietary optical design files. Core customers: NVIDIA (27.6% revenue), Cisco (18.2% revenue), Lumentum, Coherent, II-VI. Growth driver: AI datacenter optical interconnects (800G/1.6T silicon photonics).

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Revenue: $3.1B FY2026 → ~$4.0B FY2028 (AI datacom-driven)
- ROIC: ~42% today → maintain 38–45%
- Datacom revenue: ~55% of mix today → 65%+ by FY2028
- 1.6T transceiver ramp: EOY 2026 = first meaningful revenue

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
1. 1.6T transceiver ramp EOY 2026 — Fabrinet is the primary manufacturer for 800G/1.6T modules; volume ramp would drive a significant revenue step-up
2. New hyperscaler customer win beyond NVIDIA/Cisco — reduces concentration risk and would re-rate the stock
3. CEO Grady open-market purchase — the single most important signal for this stock given the insider selling overhang

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price: ~$662 (2026-04-14). GREEN band entry: $440–$500 (~33% below current). Kill Switch triggered: stock up ~150% in 3 months. Analyst consensus target $541–544 is BELOW current price.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
NVIDIA or Cisco vertically integrates optical manufacturing (builds internal capacity or acquires a competitor), removing 45.8% of Fabrinet's revenue base.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Fabrinet is the invisible backbone of AI optical infrastructure — every major photonics company (Lumentum, Coherent, NVIDIA) relies on its manufacturing, yet analysts continue to apply EMS multiples to what is effectively an AI infrastructure bottleneck play. The non-compete model creates structural trust and 42% ROIC. The thesis is right; the market has caught on. The stock is up 166% YoY and at 98% of its 52W high with every insider selling — the entry window has largely closed.

## Technology
Precision optical contract manufacturing (OCM). Fabrinet builds complex optical, electronic, and electromechanical assemblies. The non-compete model: Fabrinet has a strict policy of never entering markets served by its customers. This makes it safe for companies like NVIDIA, Cisco, Lumentum, and Coherent to share their most sensitive optical design files. No competing product lines, no IP harvesting risk. Manufacturing capabilities: silicon photonics packaging, datacom transceivers (400G/800G/1.6T), telecom coherent modules, LiDAR, medical devices, automotive sensors.
