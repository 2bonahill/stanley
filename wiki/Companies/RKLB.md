# Rocket Lab USA ($RKLB) 🔶

## Part 0 — Investment Case

**Mission Signal: STRONG** — Making space accessible as a utility — not a government program — resonates deeply. Beck's personal story (self-taught, no degree, out-competed billionaires) makes this mission narrative exceptionally powerful and sticky.

### 1. Mission
Make access to space reliable, rapid, and affordable — enabling a world where satellites and space infrastructure are as dependable as terrestrial internet.

### 2. Team & Drive
Sir Peter Beck (sole founder, 2006; CEO since 2013; self-taught engineer; honorary doctorates) is one of the most compelling founder-operators in public markets. March 2026: cut salary to $1 and forfeited 392,000 unvested RSUs — publicly redirecting that capital to R&D. This is an unusual and powerful founder commitment signal. However: CEO sold 2.5M shares Sep 2025; CFO sold ~874K shares; COO sold ~45K shares. The salary cut and RSU forfeiture post-selldown should be interpreted as commitment signaling after monetization — not a contradiction, but worth noting.

### 3. Products / Services / Goals
- **Electron** — proven small-lift reusable rocket (300kg to LEO); 50+ launches; 100% mission success 2025; first-stage reuse operational
- **Neutron** — medium-lift reusable rocket (~13,000kg to LEO); architecture designed for constellation mega-deployment; first launch target late 2026
- **HASTE** — Hypersonic test vehicle; $190M DoD contract (20 launches, Mar 2026)
- **Space Systems** — spacecraft components (reaction wheels, separation systems, solar panels), Photon satellite bus, laser comms (Mynaric acquisition)
- **National backlog**: $1.85B (+73% YoY)

Beck is the only founder-engineer who built a proven orbital rocket company from scratch without a billionaire backer. Electron provides the recurring base; Neutron is the 10x growth vector.
