## Technology
Credo is a fabless semiconductor company. Core IP is in SerDes (serializer-deserializer) — the technology that converts parallel chip data to high-speed serial signals for transmission. Key product families:
- **HiWire AEC (Active Electrical Cables)** — short-reach (≤3m) copper cables with embedded CRDO SerDes chips; dominant in AI training rack-scale cabling; replaced passive copper at density that makes optical impractical at <3m.
- **Optical PAM4 DSPs** — Robin (800G) and Cardinal (1.6T, 3nm, 224G/lane) — process the PAM4 signal modulation for optical transceivers at hyperscale AI compute distances.
- **ZeroFlap Optical Transceivers** — proprietary design cutting "link flaps" (signal drops) — reliability advantage at AI cluster scale.
- **Silicon Photonics PICs (DustPhotonics)** — Israeli company (founded 2017) with differentiated PIC portfolio: 400G/800G/1.6T, roadmap to 3.2T. NPO and CPO capable. Brings the photonic integrated circuit in-house, completing the vertical stack.
