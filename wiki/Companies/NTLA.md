# Intellia Therapeutics ($NTLA) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

**Mission Signal: STRONG** — A one-time genetic edit that permanently cures a disease (vs. lifetime injections) is one of the clearest scientific propositions in medicine. The ATTR-CM thesis directly competes with daily/weekly siRNA injections.

### 1. Mission
Edit disease-causing genes directly inside the human body — a single treatment to permanently cure conditions like ATTR-CM cardiomyopathy and hereditary angioedema.

### 2. Team & Drive
John Leonard (MD-PhD) is founding CEO since 2014. Scientific co-founders include Jennifer Doudna (Nobel Prize, Berkeley), Erik Sontheimer, and Luciano Marraffini — though non-operational. Regeneron partnership (25% co-development) provides validation and cost sharing. Director Fred Cohen bought $1M open-market (Jan 2026) — key insider signal. CEO Leonard is a net seller, partially offset by Director buying.

### 3. Products / Services / Goals
- **NTLA-2001 (nex-z)** — Phase 3 MAGNITUDE for ATTR-CM; FDA hold lifted March 2026; Regeneron 25% co-development
- **NTLA-2002 (lonvoguran)** — Phase 3 HAELO for HAE; 80 patients fully enrolled; **data H1 2026** — primary near-term catalyst; BLA target H2 2026
- **Extrahepatic programs** — preclinical; muscle, lung, CNS expansion
