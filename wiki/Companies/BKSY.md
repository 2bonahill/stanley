# BlackSky Technology ($BKSY) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Provide real-time geospatial intelligence and global monitoring services through a high-revisit satellite constellation and AI-powered analytics.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Brian O'Toole (ex-GeoEye) leads a team focused on "Speed of Insight." Drive is to beat Maxar and Airbus on revisit rate, not resolution.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
- **Gen-2 Constellation** — Current 16-satellite fleet; 1-hour revisit rate.
- **Gen-3 Satellites** — 35cm resolution + sub-hour revisit; deployment 2026.
- **Spectra AI** — Automated alerting platform for military/economic signals.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
| Metric | Current | 2–3yr Target |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Revenue | $83M (FY2025) | $150M+ |
| EPS | -$0.02 (Q4 beat) | Profitable 2026 |
| Revisit Rate | ~1 hour | <15 minutes (Gen-3) |
| Gov/Commercial Mix | 80/20 | 60/40 |

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
1. **Gen-3 Satellite Deployment** — Major technical step-up; expands TAM into high-res defense.
2. **Proficiency Turn (Net Income +)** — Expected in H2 2026.
3. **Major Gov Contract Renewal** — NRO/DoD multi-year extensions.

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current: ~$10.50. GREEN band: $7–$9. EV/Revenue ~10x on 2026 growth.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
SpaceX or Planet Labs launches a competing high-revisit/high-res constellation that commoditizes BlackSky's "Speed" moat before Gen-3 achieves scale.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
BlackSky is the "Day Trader" of earth observation. While Planet (PL) looks at everything daily, BlackSky looks at specific spots every hour. This is vital for tactical defense (Ukraine/Middle East). With an EPS beat and Gen-3 coming, it is the highest-quality growth pick in the EO sector.
