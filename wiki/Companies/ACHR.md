## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Transform urban travel by replacing 60-90 minute car commutes with 10-20 minute electric air taxi flights.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Adam Goldstein (Founder CEO) — deep background in technology and talent acquisition; mission-driven focus on solving urban congestion via the Midnight aircraft.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
"Midnight" eVTOL aircraft (4 passengers + pilot). Goal: Launch commercial service in UAE and US (NYC/Chicago) by late 2026.

- Revenue: First commercial passenger revenue (Target: 2026)

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price ~$4.50. Market cap ~$4.5B.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Structural failure or significant delay in FAA Type Certification beyond 2027.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Archer is the most vertically integrated and partnership-heavy player in the eVTOL space (United, Stellantis, UAE). The market is pricing them as a "pre-revenue hype" stock, ignoring the $2B+ liquidity and manufacturing scale-up moat provided by Stellantis.
