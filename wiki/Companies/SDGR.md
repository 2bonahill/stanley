# Schrödinger, Inc. ($SDGR) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

**Mission Signal: STRONG** — Physics-based molecular simulation to design better drugs — before synthesizing them — is a clear and demonstrably valuable mission. 31 of 32 top pharma companies using the platform is the best possible market validation.

### 1. Mission
Use physics-based molecular simulation to dramatically reduce the cost and time of drug and materials discovery — enabling scientists to design and filter molecules computationally before spending money on synthesis.

### 2. Team & Drive
Ramy Farid (PhD, Caltech computational chemistry) joined 2008 and became CEO 2017. Scientific co-founder Richard Friesner (PhD, Columbia) is Scientific Chairman — non-operational but highly credible. Novartis $150M partnership (Nov 2024) validates the platform. Both CEO Farid and Director Akinsanya sold shares in March 2026 — net insider selling. No open-market buying confirmed in 12 months.

### 3. Products / Services / Goals
- **FEP+ (Free Energy Perturbation)** — physics-based simulation; embedded in 31/32 top pharma companies; SaaS licensing; ~$200M+ annual run rate
- **SGR-3515 (Wee1/PKMYT1 inhibitor)** — Phase 1 solid tumors; **data H1 2026** — primary near-term catalyst
- **SGR-1505 (MALT1 inhibitor)** — Phase 1 B-cell malignancies; 22% ORR; Fast Track for Waldenström macroglobulinemia
- **Novartis $150M partnership** — software platform + pipeline co-development

### 4. Key Metrics (2–3yr)
| Metric | Current | 2–3yr Target |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Cash | ~$300M | Novartis deal extends runway |
| Revenue TTM | $255.87M (+23.3% YoY) | $300M+ SaaS scaling |
| Software segment | ~$200M+ recurring | Growing SaaS base |
| SGR-3515 data | H1 2026 | Phase 1 efficacy signal |

### 5. Price Catalysts
1. **SGR-3515 Phase 1 data (H1 2026)** — key near-term catalyst; positive = Phase 2 plans; negative = pipeline further shrinks
2. **Software segment revenue growth** — Q2 2026 earnings; software revenue acceleration = re-rating catalyst
3. **Novartis $150M deal milestones** — episodic but high-margin

### 6. Entry Price Anchor
| Band | Price Range | Action |
|------|------------|--------|

| AMBER | $11–$15 | Tranche 1 — current zone |
| AVOID | Above $20 | Pipeline optionality priced in |

*The software business (EV/GP ~3x at current levels) arguably supports the entire market cap. Pipeline is free optionality.*

### 7. Thesis Killer
Software revenue growth plateaus as pharma consolidation reduces customer count or AI-native competitors (Isomorphic/DeepMind) displace FEP+ with foundation-model approaches — while the proprietary pipeline produces no Phase 2 efficacy data (following SGR-2921 deaths).

### 8. Investment Hypothesis
Schrödinger is the rarest type of biotech: a SaaS infrastructure moat (FEP+ embedded in 31/32 top pharma) priced at EV/GP ~3x, with clinical pipeline as free optionality. At $839M cap, the software business alone arguably justifies the valuation. SGR-3515 data H1 2026 is the re-rating event. The key risk: SGR-2921 patient deaths (Aug 2025) damaged credibility; insider selling confirms management is not accumulating. But the SaaS floor makes downside limited.

> **Compact Summary**
> **MISSION:** Physics-based molecular simulation to design drugs before synthesizing them; 31/32 top pharma = maximum market validation
> **TEAM:** PhD CEO + PhD scientific co-founder chairman; BUT net sellers; SGR-2921 patient deaths = credibility hit
> **THESIS KILLER:** AI foundation models displace FEP+; software revenue plateaus; pipeline produces no Phase 2 data
