## 🎯 Investment Thesis (2026)
AAKI is the European-domiciled flagship for ARK's autonomous technology and robotics strategy. It focuses on the "Physical AI" revolution—where foundation models meet the real world.
1. **Autonomous Mobility:** Heavy focus on the transition from Level 2+ (SuperVision) to Level 4 (Robotaxi) via **TSLA**, **MBLY**, and **WRD**.
2. **Industrial Automation:** Capitalizing on the "re-shoring" trend in manufacturing through **TER** and **PATH**.
3. **AI Infrastructure:** Providing exposure to the hardware backbone (Foundries and XPUs) via **TSM**, **AMD**, and **NVDA**.
4. **Autonomous Defense:** Modernizing kinetic warfare through loitering munitions and swarm robotics (**KTOS**, **AVAV**).
5. **Convergence with Nuclear:** Recognizing that AGI requires massive, reliable power, the fund includes next-gen nuclear plays like **OKLO**.
