## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Develop differentiated metabolic and endocrine drug candidates — focused on obesity and MASH — that offer competitive or superior efficacy vs. the GLP-1 incumbents, with VK2735 as the oral/injectable dual GLP-1/GIP platform that could be the first oral weight loss therapy with Phase 3 efficacy matching subcutaneous options.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Brian Lian, PhD in organic chemistry (University of Michigan), founder since Sep 2012 — CEO since inception. Rare founder with both deep scientific and capital markets backgrounds (former biotech analyst at SunTrust and CIBC; Amgen research scientist). CFO Greg Zante. Both sold heavily in January 2026 ($9.7M combined at $32.96–$32.90) — the most significant alignment concern detected.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Lead: VK2735 sub-q (once-weekly dual GLP-1/GIP agonist) — VANQUISH-1 and VANQUISH-2 Phase 3 both fully enrolled (Nov 2025 and Mar 2026); 78-week treatment; data expected ~2027. VK2735 oral — Phase 2 VENTURE published (12.2% weight loss, 13 weeks); Phase 3 to initiate Q3 2026. VK2735 maintenance dosing Phase 1 ongoing (data Q3 2026). Secondary: VK2809 (MASH, Phase 2b VOYAGE completed; partnering target); VK0214 (X-ALD, Phase 1b PoC).

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- VANQUISH-1/VANQUISH-2 sub-q Phase 3 data: ~2027 — primary binary event
- Oral Phase 3 initiation: Q3 2026 — de-risks the second franchise
- VK2809 partnership: potential monetization of MASH asset
- Cash position: adequate through Phase 3 (est. $675M; ~$290M annual burn)

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- Oral Phase 3 initiation Q3 2026 — triggers re-rating for the oral GLP-1 franchise
- VK2809 partnership or out-licensing announcement
- VANQUISH data readout ~2027 — the primary binary binary event

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price $34.26. Est. cash ~$675M. Cap $3.97B. EV ≈ $3.30B = ~4.9x net cash. GREEN band: $20–$25 (EV = 2–3x net cash; cash runway provides floor). Valuation basis: EV/Net Cash for pre-commercial biotech.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
VANQUISH Phase 3 data shows VK2735 sub-q weight loss efficacy or safety is materially inferior to tirzepatide (Zepbound), dissolving the differentiation thesis against Novo/Lilly incumbents.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Viking is the most advanced pure-play small-cap bet on the oral GLP-1 theme. VK2735 Phase 2 oral data (12.2% weight loss at 13 weeks) surpasses any published oral GLP-1 Phase 2 result. Both sub-q Phase 3 trials are fully enrolled — the manufacturing and enrollment risk is resolved. Analyst consensus target of $93.59 implies the market is pricing in Phase 3 failure.
