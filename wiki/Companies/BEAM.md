## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Create life-changing genetic medicines for people with serious diseases using base editing technology — a "pencil" that can rewrite single letters of the genome without causing double-strand breaks.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO John Evans (ex-Agios, Atlas Venture partner). Scientific co-founders: Feng Zhang, PhD (Broad Institute/MIT); David Liu, PhD (Harvard/Broad); J. Keith Joung, MD-PhD (MGH/Harvard). This is the "Godfather" team of precision gene editing. Evans is a highly disciplined operator with a background in successful biotech scaling.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Lead: risto-cel (BEAM-101) for Sickle Cell Disease (SCD) — BLA submission target YE 2026. BEAM-302 (Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency) — pivotal Phase 2/3 initiation planned H2 2026. BEAM-301 (GSDIa) — Phase 1/2 ongoing. Revenue model: Internal pipeline commercialization + platform licensing. Goal: First base editing therapy on market by 2027.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- BLA Approval (risto-cel): 2027 — first commercial revenue
- BEAM-302 Pivotal Data: 2027 — validates in vivo base editing moat
- Cash Position: $1.25B (as of Q4 2025) + $500M from Sixth Street — runway to 2029

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- BLA submission (YE 2026) — triggers de-risking re-rating
- BEAM-302 pivotal initiation (H2 2026) — confirms accelerated pathway for in vivo assets
- risto-cel Phase 1/2 data updates (H1 2026) — confirms safety/efficacy durability

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price ~$27.50. Market cap ~$2.72B. EV ≈ $1.47B. GREEN band: $20–$25 (EV ≈ $1B; near-cash valuation for the most advanced base editing platform).

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Off-target editing profile or safety signal (e.g., chromosomal instability) emerges in the risto-cel BLA dataset, delaying or ending the platform's path to market.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Beam is the most advanced base editing company globally. While the SCD market is competitive (CRSP, Bluebird), Beam's BEAM-302 for AATD is the real multi-billion dollar prize with a clearer competitive moat. At a $2.7B market cap with $1.25B in cash, the market is barely valuing the pipeline beyond the lead asset.
