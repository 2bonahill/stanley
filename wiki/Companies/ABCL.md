# AbCellera Biologics ($ABCL) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

**Mission Signal: STRONG** — Discovering antibody drugs faster, cheaper, and with higher probability of clinical success than any prior method — ultimately getting more medicines to more patients — is a clear mission. The Beacon platform's ability to find rare antibodies in days vs. months has already produced one COVID drug.

### 1. Mission
Decode the immune system to find the best antibody drugs at unprecedented speed and scale — powering both discovery partnerships and an internal pipeline of best-in-class medicines.

### 2. Team & Drive
Carl Hansen (PhD, UBC microfluidics) co-founded AbCellera in 2012 and remains CEO, President, and Chairman with ~23% insider ownership. He has been buying shares open-market: $742K at ~$2.16 and $859K prior; Director Montalbano added $178K at $3.57 (Dec 2025). Hansen is personally putting capital at risk at current levels — the clearest alignment signal in this cohort.

### 3. Products / Services / Goals
- **Beacon optofluidic platform** — screens 10M+ antibody-secreting cells/run; decoded bamlanivimab (Eli Lilly COVID antibody); powers 100+ active partnerships
- **ABCL635 (NK3R antibody)** — Phase 2 (80-pt RCT); vasomotor symptoms (menopause hot flashes); first patients dosed Jan 2026; **data Q3 2026** — primary near-term binary catalyst
- **ABCL575** — Phase 1, immunological diseases; data end-2026
- **ABCL688, ABCL386** — IND/CTA-enabling stage
- **GMP manufacturing facility** — operational; clinical supply + potential commercial scale-up
- **Partnership royalty tail** — 100+ programs; Eli Lilly bamlanivimab royalties; Bruker settlement ($36M + future royalties)

### 7. Thesis Killer
ABCL635 fails Phase 2 or shows inferior efficacy vs. approved NK3R antagonists (fezolinetant/estetrol already on market) — collapsing the internal pipeline narrative and leaving the company dependent on a shrinking partnership royalty tail as bamlanivimab revenue fades.

### 8. Investment Hypothesis
At $1.07B cap with $700M in cash (65% of market cap), AbCellera's stock is effectively pricing the internal pipeline at $370M — a deep discount to the $8.40 analyst consensus target. The platform has been validated at scale (100+ programs; Lilly COVID antibody). CEO Carl Hansen buying at $2.16 while the stock is at $3.52 (up from his purchase price) signals that he sees value well above current levels. The 21.69% short float is a coiled spring if ABCL635 Phase 2 data is positive. This is the most cash-rich speculative entry in the cohort.
