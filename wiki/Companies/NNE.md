# Nano Nuclear Energy (NNE) ⚠️

## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
To provide clean, portable, and on-demand nuclear energy for the most critical infrastructure, including AI data centers and remote military outposts.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Led by Founder Jay Jiang Yu and CEO James Walker (nuclear physicist). Yu is a serial entrepreneur focused on vertical integration (fuel + transport + reactor).

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
KRONOS (1-20 MW microreactor) for off-grid power; ODIN (low-pressure reactor); LOKI (space MMR); and HALEU fuel transport/enrichment services.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- NRC Construction Permit approval (Expected April 2027).
- First physical prototype testing at University of Illinois (2027).
- First HALEU transport contract signed.

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- NRC CPA submission (Achieved April 2, 2026).
- DOE GAIN Voucher results (collaborative research at ORNL).
- MOU to contract conversion for UAE/South Korea data centers.

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current ~$45. GREEN band entry: $25–$35 (on pullback after current hype).

**7. THESIS KILLER**
NRC rejection of the KRONOS Construction Permit or a safety event during early prototype testing.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
NNE is the "portable battery" play for the AI power crisis. While large SMRs take a decade, NNE's microreactors are designed for faster licensing and modular deployment, targeting the immediate power-starved data center market.
