## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Build and deploy the world's most powerful trapped-ion quantum computers — and the broader quantum stack of networking, sensing, and security — to solve enterprise and national security problems that classical computers cannot.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Niccolò de Masi (physicist, Cambridge Cavendish, took over Feb 2025 — non-founder). Chief Scientific Advisor Christopher Monroe, PhD (co-founder, trapped-ion pioneer, 25+ years research). Christopher Ballance, PhD (President Quantum Computing, Oxford trapped-ion expert). Mihir Bhaskar, PhD (SVP Global R&D). Scientific depth is exceptional; CEO transition is the key risk. $334M insider selling by former CEO and Chair (Jun 2025) is the most negative signal detected.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Current deployed systems: Forte (5th gen) on AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and direct enterprise. 256-qubit 6th-gen system demonstrating 2026 (Cambridge University, Horizon Quantum purchase signed). SkyWater Technology acquisition (semiconductor fab) pending — enables in-house quantum chip production. Quantum networking via Lightsynq Technologies subsidiary. Defense: Missile Defense Agency SHIELD IDIQ ($151B ceiling). IonQ Italy (European expansion). Revenue goal: $225–245M in FY2026, first pure-play quantum company to sustain >$100M annually.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Revenue FY2026: $225–245M guided (+73–88% YoY); FY2027 extrapolation: $375–450M
- Backlog: $370M (as of Q4 2025 results); should reach $600M+ by YE 2026
- 6th-gen system deployed customers: ≥5 paying enterprise/government customers by YE 2026
- SkyWater acquisition close: 2026 (pending regulatory approval)

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- 256-qubit 6th-gen system commercial deployment (2026) — hardware milestone that validates trapped-ion scalability narrative against IBM/Google superconducting path
- SkyWater acquisition close — vertical integration of quantum chip production is a structural cost and IP moat compounding event
- Q2 2026 earnings — first post-6th-gen revenue confirmation; $235M guidance mid-year progress

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price $28.79. Down 66% from 52W high $84.64. 52W Low $23.49. EV ≈ $10.26B (cap $10.56B – est. cash ~$300M). GREEN band: $20–$25 (EV/Revenue÷CAGR approaches 1.0x on sustainable CAGR). Valuation basis: EV/Rev÷CAGR (Growth Track).

**7. THESIS KILLER**
IonQ's 256-qubit 6th-gen system fails to demonstrate fault-tolerant performance advantages over superconducting competitors (IBM, Google) in real enterprise benchmarks by 2027, collapsing the trapped-ion premium narrative and the entire revenue guidance at $10.56B cap.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
IonQ is the only publicly traded pure-play trapped-ion quantum company with $130M in real, recurring government and enterprise revenue — a fundamentally different category from pre-revenue quantum speculation. The market lumps all quantum stocks together and punished IONQ with a 66% decline from its 52W high. At $28.79, the stock is 8.7% from its 52W low despite having: (1) world-record gate fidelity, (2) $370M backlog, (3) $225–245M FY2026 revenue guidance, and (4) the only quantum company with multi-cloud commercial deployments at scale.
