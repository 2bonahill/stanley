## Technology
**Pegasus™ Platform — Targeted Protein Degradation (TPD):**
Heterobifunctional small molecules that bind a disease target protein and an E3 ubiquitin ligase simultaneously — causing the ligase to tag the target for destruction by the cell's proteasome. Unlike inhibitors, degraders eliminate the protein entirely. The molecule is catalytic (recycled after each event), so sub-stoichiometric doses achieve sustained pathway suppression.

**Advantages vs. biologics (e.g., dupilumab injection):**
- Oral small molecule, once daily dosing
- Attacks transcription factors (STAT6, IRF5) — historically "undruggable"
- Catalytic mechanism: smaller doses, greater depth of target reduction
