## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Build the world's most comprehensive daily view of Earth — from space — and transform that data into intelligence for governments, enterprises, and scientists; and simultaneously build the orbital AI compute infrastructure layer for the next wave of space-native intelligence.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Will Marshall, PhD in physics (Oxford), co-founder since inception, still operational. CFO Ashley Johnson (President & CFO). Co-Founder/CSO Robert Schingler. Founder-PhD CEO with co-founder CSO is the ideal founding team composition. The Suncatcher vision (orbital AI datacenters) appears personally driven — Marshall tweets regularly about the mission in real-time. All three executives are actively selling stock via 10b5-1 plans at current prices — the principal alignment risk.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Dove/Pelican satellite constellation (~200+ satellites): daily 3m/50cm resolution global imaging. Analytic platform: Planet Insights, Planetary Variables — time-series geospatial SaaS. Project Suncatcher: orbital AI datacenters (Google TPU + Nvidia Vera Rubin Space-1 chips, solar-powered, Phase 1 = 2 test satellites by early 2027). Defense & intelligence segment growing +50% YoY via sovereign contracts (DoD SHIELD IDIQ, Germany, Sweden, NATO). Goal: $415–440M FY2027 revenue (first FCF-positive fiscal year).

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Revenue FY2027: $415–440M (company guided; +35–43% YoY from $307.7M FY2026)
- FCF positive: FY2027 (first time in company history)
- NDR: ≥120% by FY2027 (from 116% FY2026 as defense expansion compounds)
- Suncatcher Phase 1: 2 test satellites launched by early 2027 — proving orbital compute economics

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- Suncatcher Phase 1 satellite launch (early 2027) — first proof of orbital AI compute viability
- Q2 2027 FCF positive confirmation — changes valuation framework
- Gen 2 Pelican launch later 2026 (30cm resolution) — technical milestone demonstrating platform improvement

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price $33.19. 52W High $38.21. EV ≈ $11.82B (cap $12.0B + $460M debt – $640M cash). GREEN band: $15–$20 (EV/Rev÷CAGR <0.5x; EV/GP <30x). Not in entry territory at current price. Valuation basis: EV/Rev÷CAGR (Growth Track).

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Project Suncatcher Phase 1 test satellites underperform — demonstrating that orbital AI compute economics are not viable at the power-to-compute ratios Planet proposes — and Planet reverts to a ~$300M revenue Earth-observation business worth $2–3B.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Planet is the only company in the world repositioning a large-scale satellite constellation into an AI compute infrastructure platform with Google and Nvidia as co-developers. The Earth observation business is profitable and growing (+26% YoY, $900M backlog). Suncatcher is a $100B+ optionality that is barely mentioned in analyst models. Insiders are actively selling at these levels, which is a powerful signal.
