# Twist Bioscience (TWST) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
To provide the world's most efficient and scalable DNA synthesis platform, enabling the next generation of biopharma, agriculture, and data storage.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Led by Founder-CEO Emily Leproust, a pioneer in silicon-based DNA synthesis. Driven by operational excellence and a "TSMC of DNA" mentality.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Synthetic genes, oligo pools, NGS library prep, and antibody discovery. Long-term goal: DNA Data Storage commercialization.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Revenue growth >20% CAGR.
- Gross margin expansion to >60%.
- Positive Adjusted EBITDA (Target 2026).

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- Profitability turnaround in FY2026.
- Commercial launch of next-gen library prep kits.
- DNA Data Storage tech transfer or partnership.

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current ~$45. GREEN band entry: $35–$40.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Failure to reach profitability in 2026 or emergence of a more efficient synthesis technology (e.g., enzymatic) that bypasses silicon.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Twist is the infrastructure layer of the synthetic biology revolution. Unlike Ginkgo, Twist sells real physical products with expanding margins. It is currently mispriced due to sector-wide "Synbio fatigue," but its unit economics are best-in-class.

---

### Compact Summary

**MISSION** — Scalable silicon-based DNA synthesis for biopharma and data storage.

**TEAM** — Emily Leproust (Founder-CEO). High operational discipline and deep domain expertise.

**THESIS KILLER** — Failure to hit 2026 profitability or technical obsolescence of silicon synthesis.
