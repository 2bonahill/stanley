# Quantum Computing Inc. ($QUBT) 🔴

## Part 0 — Investment Case

**Mission Signal: UNCLEAR** — Photonic quantum computing and TFLN foundry operations serve a real need, but the serial acquisition history and near-zero revenue make the mission narrative difficult to evaluate clearly.

### 1. Mission
Build practical quantum computers using photonic architecture and own the TFLN foundry infrastructure needed to manufacture next-generation quantum and classical photonic chips.

### 2. Team & Drive
CEO Dr. Yuping Huang (PhD quantum physics; founded QPhoton acquired by QCi; confirmed permanent CEO Jan 2026) is the technical architect of the current strategy. Holds ~21M shares. However: sold 1,000,000 shares ($14M) in September 2025 — a massive insider sell relative to company size. 0 insider buys, 4 sells on record in last 5 years. This is the dominant negative signal.

### 7. Thesis Killer
The TFLN foundry fails to secure external commercial fabrication contracts — remaining an internal cost center — while the EQC fails to demonstrate quantum advantage over classical solvers, leaving $682K revenue and a $2.4B+ market cap with no credible monetization path.

> **Compact Summary**
> **MISSION:** Photonic quantum computers + TFLN foundry infrastructure
> **TEAM:** PhD founder-CEO via acquisition; BUT sold $14M of stock; 0 insider buys ever; focus unclear from serial M&A
> **THESIS KILLER:** TFLN foundry lands no external customers; EQC shows no quantum advantage; $682K revenue, large cap
