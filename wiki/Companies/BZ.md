## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
To improve the efficiency of matching people and jobs through technology.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Founder-led by Zhao Peng (CEO). Proven ability to navigate regulatory shifts in China and scale the BOSS Zhipin model.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
BOSS Zhipin app (Direct chat recruitment), AI Agents (Screening automation). Goal: 40M+ new verified users in 2026 and expansion of "closed-loop" AI monetization.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Revenue: RMB 12B+ (2025: ~RMB 8B)
- Adjusted Operating Margin: 45%+ (Q4 25: 43.3%)
- MAU: 100M+

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- Q1 2026 Earnings (May 2026)
- New Dividend payout announcement (H2 2026)

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
$13.00 (52W Low)

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Structural youth unemployment in China leading to a collapse in paying enterprise customers (recruiters).

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Kanzhun is the "AI layer" of the Chinese labor market. While macro headwinds persist, its dominance in blue-collar digitalization and successful monetization of AI screening agents create a high-margin moat. The company's pivot to returning 50%+ of net income to shareholders via buybacks and dividends provides a valuation floor.

### Compact Summary

**MISSION** — Revolutionize recruitment matching through a mobile-first, AI-driven direct chat model.

**TEAM** — Zhao Peng (Founder/CEO), a tactical operator who successfully navigated the 2021–2022 regulatory freeze.

**THESIS KILLER** — Permanent "wage moderation" in China that destroys the incentive for companies to pay for premium recruitment tools.
