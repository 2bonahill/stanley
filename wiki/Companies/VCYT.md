## 🔬 Investment Case (Distilled)

**1. MISSION**
Transforming the cancer patient journey through genomic diagnostics that replace invasive procedures with molecular clarity.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Marc Stapley (Illumina heritage) is prioritizing operational efficiency and high-margin recurring testing. The appointment of Kevin Haas as CDTO in March 2026 signals a focus on the MRD product pipeline.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
- **Decipher:** Leader in prostate cancer genomics.
- **Afirma:** Dominant thyroid nodule diagnostic.
- **TrueMRD (Catalyst):** Minimal Residual Disease platform launching Summer 2026.
- **Goal:** $570M–$582M revenue for 2026 with ~25% Adj. EBITDA margin.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- **Revenue:** $800M+ (MRD ramp-up).
- **Gross Margin:** 75% (Product mix shift).
- **Adjusted EBITDA:** $200M+.

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- **Q1 2026 Earnings (May 5):** Confirmation of testing volume momentum.
- **TrueMRD U.S. Launch (Summer 2026):** First commercial validation of the MRD platform.
- **Prosigna LDT Launch (2026):** Expansion of breast cancer testing utility.

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
- **Current:** $34.30
- **AMBER Entry:** $34.00 (Tranche 1 - deploy now)
- **GREEN Entry:** $29.00 (Tranche 2 - set alert)

**7. THESIS KILLER**
TrueMRD launch failure or significant reimbursement denial in the prostate cancer MRD space.

**8. HYPOTHESIS**
Veracyte is evolving from a single-test diagnostics company into a high-margin clinical platform. The exit of lower-margin international businesses and the expansion into the high-growth MRD space create a valuation disconnect that the market is currently ignoring due to technical weakness below the 200DMA.
