## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Deliver on the promise of prime editing to cure a broad range of diseases by precisely correcting almost any type of genetic mutation without double-strand breaks or donor DNA.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Keith Gottesdiener, MD (ex-Rhythm, ex-Merck). Scientific co-founder David Liu, PhD (Broad Institute/Harvard). Liu invented prime editing; Gottesdiener is a veteran clinician-operator. The focus on PM359 (CGD) as the fastest path to proof-of-concept shows high clinical pragmatism.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Lead: PM359 (ex vivo prime editing for Chronic Granulomatous Disease) — Phase 1/2 ongoing; FDA indicated 2-patient data may support BLA. Pipeline: 18+ programs across liver, lung, and muscle indications. Strategic partner: Bristol Myers Squibb (BMS) for CAR-T. Goal: First prime editing BLA submission H1 2026.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- BLA submission (PM359): H1 2026 — primary binary event
- First In Vivo IND: 2026 — validates system-wide delivery
- Cash: $191.4M (as of Dec 2025) — runway through H1 2026; capital raise imminent

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- BLA submission for PM359 (H1 2026) — massive de-risking event
- IND clearance for Wilson Disease or Lung programs (2026)
- New major Pharma partnership for the Prime stack

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price ~$6.80. Market cap ~$607M. EV ≈ $415M. GREEN band: $4–$5 (EV < $300M; near-floor valuation ahead of first prime editing BLA).

**7. THESIS KILLER**
FDA rejects the 2-patient dataset for PM359, requiring a full pivotal Phase 3, which the company cannot fund without massive dilution.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Prime Medicine is the only company in the world with the "Search-and-Replace" genetic editing technology. If David Liu's prime editing is the "ultimate" CRISPR technology, PRME at a $600M cap is profoundly undervalued. The binary event is the BLA submission: if accepted, PRME re-rates to $2B+; if rejected, it likely collapses.
