# AeroVironment (AVAV) ⚠️

## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Provide the "eyes and ears" and "surgical strike" capabilities for the modern battlefield via small, autonomous robotic systems.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Wahid Nawabi (CEO) — long-tenured executive focused on scaling the Switchblade (loitering munition) and Puma (UAS) platforms as the standard for tactical US and allied defense.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
- **Switchblade 300/600**: Loitering munitions (kamikaze drones).
- **Puma/Raven**: Small unmanned aircraft systems (SUAS).
- **Goal**: Integrate the "BlueHalo" acquisition (counter-UAS) to become the primary drone/counter-drone platform for the DoD.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Switchblade revenue growth > 30% CAGR.
- Gross margin expansion to > 40% post-BlueHalo integration.
- International revenue share > 25%.

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- "Replicator" program funding allocations (2026).
- Major international orders for Switchblade 600 (Ukraine/Taiwan/Europe).
- BlueHalo integration milestones showing software-driven margin expansion.

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price ~$280. Market cap ~$9.0B.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Loss of "Program of Record" status for Switchblade or Puma to a low-cost competitor (e.g., Red Cat).

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
AVAV is the "bellwether" for the drone revolution. While trading at a high multiple, its integration into the US defense budget's "Program of Record" provides a durable moat that newer startups lack.
