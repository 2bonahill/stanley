# Redwire ($RDW) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Provide the mission-critical infrastructure, components, and services that enable the burgeoning space economy — from satellite manufacturing to in-space 3D printing.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Peter Cannito (ex-McKinsey, ex-CEO Polaris Alpha) leads an operator-heavy team. Redwire was formed through the consolidation of several heritage space firms (Made In Space, Adcole, Roccor), giving it a diverse but un-unified engineering culture. Drive is focused on becoming the "TSMC of space infrastructure."

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
- **Satellite Infrastructure** — Solar arrays (iROSA for ISS/Gateway), sensors, and deployment mechanisms.
- **In-Space Manufacturing** — 3D printing of high-value materials (ZBLAN optical fiber) in microgravity.
- **Defense/UAS** — Stalker UAS platforms for Navy/Marines.
- **Artemis II** — Vital navigation and imaging tech integrated into the Orion capsule.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
| Metric | Current | 2–3yr Target |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Revenue | $305M TTM | $500M+ (organic growth focus) |
| Gross Margin | 24% | 30%+ via manufacturing mix |
| Net Income | Negative (-$0.35 EPS) | EBITDA positive 2026 |
| Backlog | ~$300M+ | $600M+ |

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
1. **Artemis II Success** — Hardware validation on a historic, high-visibility human mission.
2. **ESA QKDSat Launch** — First mover in quantum-secure satellite communications infrastructure.
3. **EBITDA Inflection** — Q1/Q2 2026 earnings confirming transition to profitability.

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current: ~$18.20. GREEN band: $14–$16. EV/Revenue ~1.5x on 2026 guide is the target anchor.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Organic revenue growth stalls below 10%, revealing Redwire as a collection of stagnant, high-CapEx legacy components businesses rather than a high-growth platform.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Redwire is the picks-and-shovels play for the "SpaceX IPO" hype cycle. While launch providers are binary and capital-intensive, Redwire's flight heritage (Artemis, iROSA) makes it the default supplier for everyone else. At <2x revenue with growing defense orders, it is a high-conviction infrastructure bet.
