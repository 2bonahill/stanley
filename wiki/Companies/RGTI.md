# Rigetti Computing (RGTI) ⚠️

## Part 0 — Investment Case

> **BELLWETHER — SCORING OPTIONAL**

### Compact Summary

**MISSION** — Building the world's most scalable quantum computing platform using a modular chiplet-based architecture.

**TEAM** — Subodh Kulkarni (CEO). Founder Chad Rigetti transitioned to a technical/advisory role and recently launched Sygaldry (AI-quantum).

**THESIS KILLER** — Failure of the modular chiplet architecture to achieve >99.9% fidelities at scale, losing out to trapped-ion or neutral-atom modalities.

## Technology & Moat
- **Modular Chiplet Architecture**: Allows tiling of smaller, high-yield quantum chips into larger arrays.
- **Speed**: Superconducting qubits are 1,000x faster than trapped-ion, critical for real-time error correction and AI integration.
- **NVIDIA Integration**: Deeply integrated with NVIDIA's "Ising" quantum-AI software stack.
