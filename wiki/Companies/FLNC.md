## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Build and operate the world's leading grid-scale battery energy storage platform — combining BESS hardware, software intelligence (Fluence IQ), and services — to enable the global energy transition.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
CEO Julian Nebreda (from AES, one of Fluence's founding shareholders). Track B operator — not founder-led. AES + Siemens jointly own ~64% of shares — strategic control risk. No open-market insider buying. Former director sold $165K worth of shares March 2026.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Fluence is a grid-scale BESS (Battery Energy Storage System) integrator. Key distinction: it designs, procures, and integrates storage systems using third-party LFP cells, rather than making batteries itself. Revenue split: ~85% hardware/EPC project revenue + ~15% software ARR (Fluence IQ: Mosaic bidding optimization + Nispera O&M). Domestic content moat: LFP cells manufactured in Tennessee (POSCO HY Clean Energy) qualify for 10% ITC bonus AND bypass 25% Section 301 tariffs on Chinese batteries (effective Jan 2026). Strategic shareholders: AES + Siemens (64%).

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- FY2026 revenue: $3.2–3.6B (+50% YoY from $2.4B FY2025)
- Backlog: $5.5B (FY2026 Q1) — fully covers FY2026 guidance midpoint for the first time
- Software ARR: $145M FY2025 → $180M FY2026 target
- First profitable operating year: $40–60M adj. EBITDA FY2026 (if delivered)
- Gross margin recovery: Q1 FY2026 collapsed to 4.9% — must recover to >10% by Q2

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
1. Q2 FY2026 earnings (May 2026) — gross margin recovery above 10% is the single most critical data point
2. Domestic content certification flowing through backlog — if ITC bonus + tariff exemption are confirmed across projects, margins expand structurally
3. Utility-scale storage demand acceleration — IRA, energy transition tailwinds; BESS market growing >30% CAGR

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price: ~$13.78 (2026-04-14). 52W range: $3.46–$33.51. GREEN band entry: $9–13 (at or below 200DMA $18.78; 0.35–0.45x EV/FY2026 revenue). AMBER band: $13–18.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Q2 FY2026 gross margin fails to recover above 8% — confirming the Q1 cost overruns are structural, not one-off, and making the path to EBITDA profitability implausible.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Fluence trades at ~0.49x EV/FY2026 revenue after a brutal Q1 earnings miss, but the $5.5B backlog (first time in history it fully covers annual guidance) and domestic content moat create a compelling recovery setup. The market is pricing in structural margin failure; if Q2 shows gross margin recovering above 10%, this is a trough valuation. The domestic content advantage adds a 2–3 year window before competitors replicate US-made supply chains.

## Technology
Grid-scale BESS integrator. Does NOT manufacture battery cells — sources LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) cells from POSCO HY Clean Energy (Tennessee facility, NDAA/ITC compliant). Designs and deploys complete storage systems (Sunstack platform). Software: **Fluence IQ** — two products:
1. **Mosaic**: AI-powered bidding and dispatch optimization for storage operators (ARR growing)
2. **Nispera**: O&M and performance monitoring platform

**Domestic content moat:** Tennessee-sourced LFP cells qualify for 10% ITC adder AND are exempt from 25% Section 301 tariffs on Chinese batteries (effective Jan 2026). Competitors using Chinese cells face tariffs + lose ITC adder.
