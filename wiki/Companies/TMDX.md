# TransMedics Group ($TMDX) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

**Mission Signal: STRONG** — Keeping a donated heart beating outside the body during transport, expanding the pool of transplantable organs and saving lives on waitlists, is a mission with universal moral clarity. Clinician and patient advocacy compounds automatically.

### 1. Mission
Expand the number of lives saved by organ transplantation by replacing cold storage — which damages organs during transport — with warm, perfused preservation that keeps donor hearts, lungs, and livers functioning outside the body.

### 2. Team & Drive
Dr. Waleed Hassanein (MD, cardiac surgeon) founded TransMedics in 1998 and has led it for 26+ years. He purchased $1.98M of stock open-market in August 2025 and $1M more in November 2025 — two purchases totaling ~$3M while the stock was at $113–119. Named Top 50 Global Thinkers in Healthcare (Foreign Policy, 2015); finalist for the European Patent Office Inventor Award (2017). Founder-surgeon-CEO building an organ care infrastructure company.

### 3. Products / Services / Goals
- **OCS Heart/Lung/Liver** — only FDA-approved warm perfusion platform for all three organ types
- **National OCS Program (NOP)** — 22-aircraft aviation fleet for end-to-end organ logistics; consumables recurring revenue
- **ENHANCE trial** — 650+ patient blinded RCT for OCS Heart; FDA IDE approved Feb 2026; superiority vs cold storage in DBD cases = standard-of-care expansion catalyst
- **DENOVO trial** — OCS Lung; FDA IDE approved Jan 2026
- **Kidney expansion** — next organ type in development

### 7. Thesis Killer
ENHANCE trial delivers a non-superiority result for OCS Heart vs. cold storage in DBD cases — removing the standard-of-care expansion catalyst and capping OCS adoption at the current marginal-organ niche. Business continues but growth decelerates materially.

### 8. Investment Hypothesis
The market values TMDX as a device company at 6x EV/Revenue. The correct frame is organ care infrastructure: OCS + NOP aviation fleet + recurring consumables + ENHANCE-driven standard-of-care shift. A founder-surgeon buying $3M of his own stock at $113–119 while analysts average $156 target price (+38% upside) suggests the market has not priced the ENHANCE optionality. Even without a positive ENHANCE readout, a 37%-CAGR profitable business at P/E 23.47 is rare — the ENHANCE trial is free optionality embedded in a reasonable core valuation.

## Technology
The OCS maintains donor organs in a warm, beating, metabolizing state outside the human body, replacing cold storage (which causes ischemic damage and limits transport time):
- **OCS Heart** — beats throughout transport; ENHANCE trial underway
- **OCS Lung** — ventilated during transport; DENOVO trial underway
- **OCS Liver** — metabolizing bile production maintained
- **National OCS Program (NOP)** — full logistics layer: TMDX operates 22 aircraft for end-to-end organ transport, generating recurring logistics revenue independent of device pricing
