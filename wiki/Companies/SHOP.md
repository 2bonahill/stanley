## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
To make commerce better for everyone by providing a comprehensive platform to start, grow, manage, and scale a business.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Founder-led by Tobi Lütke. Proven ability to pivot (e.g., selling logistics business to Deliverr/Flexport to focus on core software) and stay ahead of technology curves (AI, Shop Pay).

Core SaaS platform for SMBs and enterprise (Shopify Plus). Goals: Standardizing commerce via the Universal Commerce Protocol and integrating AI agents (Sidekick) across all merchant workflows.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Revenue: $20B+
- FCF Margin: 20%+
- GMV: $500B+

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**

$110-$120 (Reference only)

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Shopify is the dominant "non-Amazon" commerce rail. Its shift to a high-margin, asset-light software model combined with deep AI integration makes it a mandatory reference for the commerce infrastructure sector.
