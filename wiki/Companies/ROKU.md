## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
To be the operating system for all TV, connecting the entire TV ecosystem.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Founder-led by Anthony Wood. High "say-do" ratio on operational scale, though reporting transparency is decreasing in 2025.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
Streaming players, Roku-branded TVs, and the Roku OS. Goal: 100M+ households and sustainable profitability.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- Revenue: ~$5.5B
- Growth: 15–20%
- Operating Income: Positive (2026 target)

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- Q1 2026 Earnings (2026-05)
- Expansion of Roku Ads Manager adoption (2026)

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
$95.00

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Market share in the US dropping below 30% due to Samsung/Amazon/Vizio(Walmart) pressure.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
The shift from linear to connected TV (CTV) is inevitable. Roku's scale (90M households) makes it a critical gatekeeper for advertisers, despite hardware competition.
