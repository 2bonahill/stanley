# Iridium Communications (IRDM) 🟢

## Part 0 — Investment Case

### Full Investment Case

**1. MISSION**
Provide the world's only truly global satellite network for mission-critical voice and data communications.

**2. TEAM & DRIVE**
Matthew Desch (CEO) — long-tenured leader who took Iridium from bankruptcy to a highly profitable, cash-flowing LEO constellation leader.

**3. PRODUCTS, SERVICES & GOALS**
66 LEO satellites. Services: Certus broadband, IoT, PNT (Positioning, Navigation, and Timing). Goal: Expand into "NTN Direct" (Direct-to-Device) for smartphones.

**4. KEY METRICS IN 2–3 YEARS**
- FCF Yield > 10%
- IoT Subscriber growth > 15% CAGR
- First commercial revenue from NTN Direct (2026)

**5. PRICE CATALYSTS**
- NTN Direct smartphone launch (H2 2026)
- Major defense contract for "PNT" (alternative to GPS)
- Dividend increases / Share buybacks (funded by $300M+ FCF)

**6. ENTRY PRICE ANCHOR**
Current price ~$28. Market cap ~$4.0B.

**7. THESIS KILLER**
Failure of the "NTN Direct" initiative to gain traction against Starlink/ASTS.

**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
Iridium is the "safe" way to play the space economy. Unlike RKLB or PL, it is already FCF positive. The market is ignoring its role as the primary backup to GPS (PNT) and its high-margin IoT recurring revenue.

---

### Compact Summary

**MISSION** — Provide truly global satellite communications for mission-critical IoT, aviation, and defense applications.

**TEAM** — Matthew Desch (CEO); proven track record of LEO operational excellence; execution risk is competition from Starlink.

**THESIS KILLER** — Failure of the "NTN Direct" (smartphone) initiative to gain traction.
