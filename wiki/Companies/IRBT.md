**8. INVESTMENT HYPOTHESIS**
iRobot is a classic "innovator's dilemma" victim. Its legacy vacuum business has been commoditized by better-capitalized Chinese rivals (Roborock, Dreame) that offer superior LiDAR and base-station technology at lower prices. Without the Amazon umbrella, iRobot lacks the R&D budget or data ecosystem to pivot to general-purpose robotics.
