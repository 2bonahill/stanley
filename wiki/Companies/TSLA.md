# Tesla (TSLA) 🔶🔴

## Part 0 — Investment Case

> **MEGA CAP REFERENCE — NO SCORING**

### Compact Summary

**MISSION** — Accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy through electric vehicles, energy storage, and autonomous AI.

**TEAM** — Elon Musk (CEO), Vaibhav Taneja (CFO), Tom Zhu (SVP Automotive). Musk's multi-company AI ecosystem (xAI, SpaceX) provides a unique talent and compute moat.

**THESIS KILLER** — Failure to achieve unsupervised FSD (Full Self-Driving) or humanoid robot (Optimus) commercialization, reducing the company to a capital-intensive automotive manufacturer.

## Key Catalysts (2026-2027)
- **FSD EU/China Approval**: Dutch regulators (RDW) seeking EU-wide approval for FSD.
- **Robotaxi Event/Launch**: Continued progress toward a dedicated autonomous ride-hailing platform.
- **Terafab Project**: Massive AI chip complex involving SpaceX and Tesla to scale AI5 and beyond.
- **Optimus Gen 3**: Deployment of humanoid robots in Tesla factories for real-world validation.

## Technology & Moat
- **AI Infrastructure**: Dojo supercomputer and massive real-world video dataset from millions of vehicles.
- **Vertical Integration**: In-house chip design (AI5), battery chemistry, and manufacturing automation.
- **Energy Ecosystem**: Leading grid-scale storage (Megapack) with high margins and growing backlog.
